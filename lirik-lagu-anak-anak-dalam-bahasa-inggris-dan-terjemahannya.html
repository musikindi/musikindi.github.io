sifat anak buaya

Crocodiles can move very quickly at short distances, even outside water. This animal has a very strong jaw, which can bite with extraordinary strength, making it the animal with the greatest bite strength. This crocodile bite pressure is not less than 5,000 psi (pounds per square inch; equivalent to 315 kg / cm²) compare with the strength of a bite dog that is only 335 psi rottweiler, a giant white shark of 400 psi, or dub (hyena) around 800 - 1,000 psi. Sharp crocodile teeth and sharp, very useful for holding prey. The crocodile attacks its prey by pouncing and biting its prey, then pulls it firmly and suddenly into the water. Therefore the muscles around the jaw develop so well that they can close very strongly. Mouth that has closed so is also very difficult to open, similar to gecko bites. But on the contrary, the muscles that function to open the crocodile's mouth are very weak. The crocodile researchers simply wrapped a large adhesive tape (duct tape) several times or tied a rubber band inside the end of the snout that closes, to keep the mouth closed while observing and measuring, or when wanting to transport the animal safely. Crocodile's claws and nails are strong and sharp, but the neck is very stiff so the crocodile is not so easy to attack sideways or backward
