38. No, there isn't / No, there aren''t artinya apa ya dan apa perbedaannya?​


Jawaban:

Perbedaannya terletak pada subjeknya.

Is = He, she, it

Are = You, we, they

Penjelasan:

Contohnya:

A: He is your father, isn't he?

B: Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.

A: They play tennis, aren't they?

B: Yes, they are. /No, they aren't.

Semoga membantu.

Jawaban:

Arti No, there isn't / No, there aren't = Tidak, tidak ada

Perbedaanya =

No, there isn't : Untuk Subject Yang Singular

No, there aren't : Untuk Subject Yang Plural
