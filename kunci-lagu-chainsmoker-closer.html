prettier = lebih cantik

writing = menulis

Jawaban:

Jawaban:

1. Tangga nada diatonis mayor merupakan tangga nada yang memiliki interval (jarak nada) 1 1 ½ 1 1 1 ½. Apabila dimainkan, tangga nada mayor mempunyai nuansa yang ceria dan menyenangkan.

2. Adagio: tempo lambat tapi santai.

Lento: tempo lebih lambat dan perlahan.

Largo: tempo sangat lambat.

Allegro: tempo cepat.

Moderato: tempo sedang (tidak lambat dan tidak cepat)

Andante: tempo lambat - sedang.

Vivace: tempo lebih cepat dan lincah.

Presto: tempo sangat cepat.

17. Apa makna dari lagu closer


“Closer” is the story of a romance that dissolved in the past. But now, years later, it has been resurrected as a result of a chance meeting between the two participants. Upon seeing each other, the former lovers realize that they are still interested in each other and decide to hookup. References made throughout the song, like those pointing to actual places, are not symbolic but recalls of the songwriters’ past.
