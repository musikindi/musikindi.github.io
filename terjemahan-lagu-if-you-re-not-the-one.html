Pembahasan:

Untuk Pembahasan:

Untuk mengecek kebenaran suatu informasi, kita menggunakan expression of asking for certainty. Asking for certainty adalah jenis expression yang biasa digunakan untuk mengonfimasi kebenaran suatu informasi.

berikut beberapa ekspresi untuk asking for certainty:

- are you sure?

- are you certain?

- really?

- do you think so?

- is it confirmed?

berikut beberapa ekspresi untuk merespon asking for certainty:

certainty

ekspresi-ekspresi berikut ini digunakan apabila penjawab yakin atas kebenaran informasi yang ditanyakan.

- of course

- I'm sure about it

- I have no doubt about it

- I'm absolutely sure about it

- I'm absolutely certain that ...

- Yes, I'm certain

- I'm sure of it

- Without doubt

- I'm positive

uncertainty

ekspresi-ekspresi berikut ini digunakan apabila penjawab tidak yakin atas kebenaran informasi yang ditanyakan.

- I'm not sure about it

- I don't think so

- I don't know yet

- I doubt it

- I am not a hundred percent sure

- I'm not really sure about ...

- There's surely some doubt in my mind that ...

- I'm not certain about ...

Berikut dialog untuk situasi diatas:

Your friend: Here is the vegetable soup recipe that you asked.

You: Thanks, buddy. Can i see it first?

Your friend: sure.

You: Hey, are you sure that the cooking time is 25 minutes? I think that's too long because the potato and the carrot will be too soft.

Your friend: Yes, I'm sure about it because you will use low heat when cooking it.

You: I see. Thank you for the recipe.

Your friend: You're welcome
